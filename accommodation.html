Find Hotels

We have arranged special conference prices with hotels in the inner city of Aachen. The city centre is not very big, making Aachen a very "walkable" city. Most of the hotels recommended below are within walking distance of the central station (Aachen Hauptbahnhof). The conference venue can be reached from most of these hotels within 20 minutes.

If you are booking one of our recommended hotels, please book directly with the respective hotelM and use the respective reference to get the reduced rates! The hotel contingents are limited. Most contingents expire mid August.
