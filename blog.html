Latest Updates

Change of Location: Conference is being held in the Lecture hall TEMP - see Venue.

Media Psychology 21 will be held in a hybrid format. In person attendence is limited to 77 attendees.

Information for in-person attendees: Please bring a certificate of your 3G status (vaccinated, recovered, tested). As an extra measure of safety, vaccinated and recovered attendees should additionally be tested. Please come with a valid test (as of tested in the last 48h). Alternatively, we will provide tests at the registration.

Virtual attendence includes streaming of all talks (over Zoom) with the possibility to ask questions and asynchronous interaction with poster presenters (via padlet).
